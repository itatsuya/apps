Metal Imprinting Area

When you create a template dedicated for use with metal imprinting, one metal imprinting area (80 mm × 80 mm; 3.2 in. × 3.2 in.) and one edit area (50 mm × 50 mm; 2.0 in. × 2.0 in.) are created in the center of the template.


  A: Metal imprinting area
  B: Edit area

The metal imprinting area indicates the metal imprinting range within the template.
If you drag this area to move it, the metal imprinting position relative to the target changes.
: Turn this on when you do not want to change the object's position or size.

Lay out images, characters, and edit areas within the metal imprinting area. If any items are outside of this area, an error will occur and you will not be able to save the template.
Sometimes, it is best to limit the object layout positions depending on the model you are using. One such example is when it is possible for the metal imprinting results to be indistinct even though the items are all inside the metal imprinting area.
For details, refer to the documentation for the model you are using.
