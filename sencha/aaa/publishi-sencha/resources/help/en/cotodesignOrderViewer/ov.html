Order Status

1. Unpaid orders
This is a list of the orders that have not yet been paid for.
Once payment has been received, click  .
When payment is received for an order, it is moved to the "2. Waiting for printing" field and can be printed with cotodesign Print Manager.

  1. Click an order for which payment has been received.
  2. View the order information at the bottom of the screen and confirm that this is the customer's order.
  3. Click  .
This status is enabled when the "Skip Payment Confirmation" check box is cleared on the [Folder Path] tab of the "Settings" screen in cotodesign Print Manager.

2. Waiting for printing
This is the list of orders that are waiting to be printed.
When you use cotodesign Print Manager to proceed to the printing processing, the orders move to the "3. Sent for printing" field.

3. Sent for printing
This is the list of orders that are being printed or that have been printed.
Once the product has been delivered to the customer, click  .
When orders are set to delivered, they move to the "4. Delivered" field.

  1. Click an order for which the product has been delivered.
  2. View the order information at the bottom of the screen and confirm that this is the customer's order.
  3. Click  .

4. Delivered
This is the list of orders that have been delivered to customers, thereby completing all the processing.
